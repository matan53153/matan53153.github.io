## [Impact of salinity and phosphorus on growth of Phaseolus Vulgaris inoculated with Arbuscular Mycorrhizal Fungi](https://emerginginvestigators.org/articles/21-157)

During my time in high school, I became interested in environmental science and agriculture. Living in Thailand where rice farming is a huge part of the economy, I have always wondered what would happen if climate change were to displace a large number of Thai farmers. As a result, I developed an experiment, wrote, and published a paper on how fungi could increase plant growth.
